Over de site:

Our site, called the RDG (Random Dinner Generator) generates three recipes for the you, which can be personalized up to a certain extent. You can include or exclude certain ingredients: the options for which can be found in the menu. This makes sure you always have the perfect recipe ready within a few seconds, bursting with your favorite flavours and without certain ingredients you do not like or even have allergic reactions towards. The 3 recipes are presented to you in an easy format.

It could happen that a recipe does not meet our requirements, format- or otherwise: this is due to the fact that the filters for the recipes are not always perfect. Because the recipes are pulled from different sources, layouts might differ or a certain element (e.g. a specific kind of cheese) might not be included in our filters.

This site is for those who:
  • Like tasty, easy-to-make food
  • Have no idea what they want to eat today
  • Want to easily find a recipe
